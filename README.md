# BizGrowth

## Overview

BizGrowth is a B2B business growth and networking platform designed to connect Corporates, MSMEs, Business Consultants, Trade Organizations, Associations, and Government Representatives.

The platform enables businesses to discover opportunities, expand into local and global markets, collaborate with strategic partners, participate in industry events, and access expert consulting services.

Inspired by platforms such as IndiaMART and GlobalLinker, BizGrowth aims to become a unified ecosystem for business growth, innovation, and professional networking.

---

## Vision

To create a digital ecosystem that empowers businesses of all sizes to connect, collaborate, innovate, and grow in both domestic and international markets.

## Backend Architecture

The project follows a Modular Monolith Architecture.

```text
src/
│
├── config/
├── middleware/
├── routes/
├── controllers/
├── services/
├── models/
├── utils/
│
├── modules/
│   ├── auth/
│   ├── users/
│   ├── organizations/
│   ├── marketplace/
│   ├── consultants/
│   ├── events/
│   ├── content/
│   └── admin/
│
└── app.js
```
